About KiKenya

A Nation of Many Voices

As Kenya's ethnic makeup continues to evolve in the wake of modernization, KiKenya asks: how many communities still hold fast to their culture, dialects and identity in the face of modernity?

"Harambee — pulling together. Kenya's greatest strength lies in the beautiful mosaic of its peoples."

We celebrate the Bantu, Nilotic, and Cushitic peoples of Kenya — their art, language, clothing, food, music and way of life. This platform exists to document, preserve and share these stories for future generations.
